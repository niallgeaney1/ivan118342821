INTERESTS

Sports.

Football

From a very young age I have had a passion for Gaelic Football. I play football with my home club, Dingle at senior and under 21 level. I also played football with my school, Pobalscoil Chorca Dhuibhne. We won the Corn Ui Mhuiri cup last year which was a big achievement for the school. Also this year the Dingle senior team made the County Championship Final but we fell short to Dr.Crokes of Killarney. I enjoy football because it keeps the body healthy and gives you a break from the outside world. I also enjoy it because it allows you to make lots of new friends

Basketball

Over the last few years I have had a passion for basketball, in particular the NBA. I dont play the sport as there is no team available to play on but I do enjoy watching it. My favourite team in the NBA are the Oklahoma City Thunder. I enjoy watching the two superstars on that team, Russell Westbrook and Paul George. Hopefully this year the team can progress to the next level and win the championship.

Golf

Another sport in which i enjoy is Golf. I enjoy playing Pitch & Putt during the summer back at home and occasionally playing golf on the local course. I love to watch the big competitons, including the 4 major tournaments, The Augusta Masters, The US Open, The British Open and the US PGA Championship. My favourite players to watch are Tiger Woods, Ricky Fowler and Rory McIlroy.
